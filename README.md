## Online Learning Platforms

Platforms offering structured courses, certifications, and learning programs.

- [Coursera](https://www.coursera.org/) — Online courses and certifications from universities and organizations.
- [edX](https://www.edx.org/) — University-level courses and professional certificates.
- [FutureLearn](https://www.futurelearn.com/) — Online courses from universities and institutions worldwide.
- [Udemy](https://www.udemy.com/) — Marketplace for courses across a wide range of topics.

## Course Creation Platforms

Tools for creating, hosting, and selling online courses.

- [Kajabi](https://kajabi.com/) — Platform for building and selling online courses and memberships.
- [Teachable](https://teachable.com/) — Course creation and monetization platform.
- [Thinkific](https://www.thinkific.com/) — Platform for creating and delivering online courses.

## Learning Management Systems (LMS)

Systems for managing learners, content, and course delivery.

- [Canvas LMS](https://www.instructure.com/canvas) — Cloud-based LMS for education institutions.
- [Moodle](https://moodle.org/) — Open-source LMS used globally.
- [TalentLMS](https://www.talentlms.com/) — LMS platform for corporate and educational training.

## Video Learning & Hosting

Platforms for hosting, streaming, and delivering video-based learning content.

- [YouTube](https://www.youtube.com/) — Video platform widely used for educational content.
- [Vimeo](https://vimeo.com/) — Video hosting platform for professional and educational content.
- [Panopto](https://www.panopto.com/) — Video platform for lecture capture and learning.

## Language Learning

Platforms and tools for learning new languages online.

- [Duolingo](https://www.duolingo.com/) — Gamified language learning platform.
- [Babbel](https://www.babbel.com/) — Subscription-based language learning platform.
- [italki](https://www.italki.com/) — Platform connecting learners with language tutors.

## Technical & Developer Learning

Platforms focused on programming, technology, and technical skills.

- [freeCodeCamp](https://www.freecodecamp.org/) — Free coding curriculum with certifications and projects.
- [Pluralsight](https://www.pluralsight.com/) — Technology-focused learning platform for professionals.
- [Codecademy](https://www.codecademy.com/) — Interactive coding and programming courses.

## Open Education & Free Resources

Open-access platforms and resources for free learning materials.

- [Khan Academy](https://www.khanacademy.org/) — Free educational platform covering a wide range of subjects.
- [MIT OpenCourseWare](https://ocw.mit.edu/) — Free course materials from MIT.
- [OpenLearn (Open University)](https://www.open.edu/openlearn/) — Free courses and educational resources.
